Our Technology

Our fake news detector uses a regularized Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network trained on millions of verified and false articles. The system analyzes linguistic patterns, structural elements, and semantic inconsistencies that humans might miss.

PROCESS

How It Works

Advanced ML techniques to detect misinformation

Text Input & Preprocessing

Article text undergoes sophisticated preprocessing including tokenization, normalization, and feature extraction. Our system analyzes everything from sentence structure to emotional tone.

LSTM Neural Network Analysis

Our deep learning model utilizes LSTM architecture to identify patterns characteristic of fake news. It captures long-term dependencies in language while regularization techniques prevent overfitting.

Feature Analysis

The system analyzes over 150 different features, including lexical diversity, emotional manipulation markers, attribution patterns, and source credibility indicators commonly associated with fake news.

Classification & Confidence Score

Our model produces a probability score indicating how likely the article is to be fake or genuine. The confidence score reflects the certainty of the classification based on the strength of detected patterns.

Important Disclaimer

While our system uses advanced ML techniques, it should be used as one of many tools to evaluate news credibility. No automated system is perfect, and critical thinking remains essential. Always verify information from multiple reliable sources before drawing conclusions or sharing content.
